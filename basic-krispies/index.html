The Original Rice Krispies Treats Recipe

This classic, delicious snack has been making memories for kids and parents alike for generations.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 package of JET-PUFFD Marshmallow
  • 6 cups of Kellog's Rice Krispies cereal
Shop Now for Ingredients

Directions

  1. In a large saucepan, melt buttwe over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
  2. Add KELLOGG'S RICE KRIPSIES cereal. Stir until well coated
  3. Using buttered spatula or wax paper evenly press mixture into a 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan coated with cooking spray. Cool. Cut into 2-inch squares. Best if served the same day.

Notes

  • For best result, use fresh marshmallows
  • 1 jar (7 oz) marshmallow creme can be substituted for marshmallows
  • Diet, reduced calorie or tub margarine is not recommended.
  • Store no more than two days at room temp in airtight container.
  • To freeze, place in layers separated by wax paper in airtight container. Freeze for up to 6 weeks. Let stand at room temperature before serving.
